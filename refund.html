Refund Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

New paid subscriptions may request a refund within 14 days of first purchase, unless prohibited by applicable law or subject to abuse/fraud review.

Renewal charges are generally non-refundable. You can cancel before renewal to avoid future charges.

3. Team Seats

Seat adjustments take effect according to plan billing terms. Partial-period prorated refunds may not be available.

4. How to Request

Contact billing@example.com with account email, invoice ID, and reason for request.

5. Payment Processor

Refund processing times depend on the payment processor and your financial institution.
